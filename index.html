About Me

Who I Am

Building Cold Email Systems That Turn Targeted Leads Into Qualified Conversations

I'm a B2B Cold Email Specialist at Accord Tech Solutions, focused on building complete cold outreach systems from the ground up.

I don't just write cold emails — I manage the full system behind them, including domain and inbox setup, authentication, warm-up, lead sourcing, data cleaning, ICP segmentation, email sequence writing, campaign setup, deliverability monitoring, and performance optimization using tools like Instantly.ai.

My work focuses on creating a clean, scalable outreach process where every part is connected — verified data, clear targeting, strong messaging, healthy sending infrastructure, and continuous campaign improvement.

The goal is simple: better deliverability, cleaner targeting, more qualified replies, and more real B2B conversations.
